HomeTogether™ · Our Blended Care Program

Care, connection, and peace of mind, right through the TV.

There's nothing to learn, nothing to manage, and no new device to figure out. Everything appears automatically on the television in a simple, easy-to-read format, so staying connected is effortless.

Video calls through the TV

Loved ones, caregivers, and care-team members can video call right on the television, one-on-one or as a group. Easy to answer, and a real help against loneliness.

Wellness & routine reminders

On-screen reminders support medications, hydration, meals, and daily routines, helping seniors stay on track without feeling overwhelmed.

Passive safety monitoring

Non-intrusive sensors watch for motion, temperature, sound, and humidity to help detect changes in routine or possible concerns. No cameras, ever.

Optional health-device connections

HomeSight® can integrate with compatible devices like blood-pressure monitors or glucose meters, giving our care team helpful insight to support proactive care.

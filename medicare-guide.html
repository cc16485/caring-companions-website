Medicare GUIDE Program

75 hours of Medicare-covered respite care, delivered by us.

Caring Companions is an approved provider under Medicare's GUIDE Program, so we can bill Medicare directly for in-home respite care for eligible individuals enrolled in GUIDE.

These hours are designed to give unpaid family caregivers meaningful breaks, while making sure their loved one stays safe, supported, and cared for at home. The GUIDE Program focuses on:

✓ Dementia-specific care coordination
✓ Education and support for unpaid caregivers
✓ Access to respite care services, when eligible

GUIDE is built to reduce caregiver burnout, improve coordination of care, and help people living with dementia remain at home longer.

Who may be eligible?

The GUIDE Program is intended for individuals who:

✓ Have Original Medicare (Parts A & B)
✓ Have a documented dementia diagnosis
✓ Do not live in a long-term nursing facility
✓ Are not enrolled in hospice or certain Medicare Advantage plans

Good to know

The 75 respite hours don't roll over, unused hours expire June 30, and new hours become available each July 1. We can bill Medicare only after GUIDE enrollment is approved, and respite availability follows Medicare's eligibility guidelines.

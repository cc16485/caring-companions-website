About Us

Why families across Southwest Missouri trust us.

We help seniors and adults who need support keep living safely, comfortably, and with dignity in the place that means the most to them — home.

Caring Companions was founded in 2017 with a heartfelt mission: to help seniors and individuals who need support continue living safely, comfortably, and with dignity in the place that means the most to them — home.

Our journey began after seeing how overwhelming it can be for families to navigate care on their own. Too many people were being moved into facilities before they were ready — not because they couldn't live at home, but because they didn't have the right support. We created Caring Companions to change that, by offering compassionate, dependable, relationship-centered in-home care that truly honors each person's life, story, and independence.

Today, our team provides non-medical home care for those who need temporary or long-term help with daily living. Families trust us for flexible support — from hourly visits to live-in and 24-hour care — delivered by caregivers who are kind, respectful, patient, and deeply committed to the people they serve.

We believe great care is personal. We take time to understand each client's routines, preferences, and goals so care feels supportive, never intrusive. Our caregivers don't just assist with tasks — they provide companionship, encouragement, and the reassurance of knowing someone is truly there for your loved one.

Our Employees

Every caregiver is thoroughly screened, insured, and bonded — and they're our employees, not contractors. We handle the taxes, insurance, and backup support whenever it's needed.

Family Owned

We're not a franchise. Caring Companions is family owned, and our experience, compassion, and quality of care run higher because of that personal touch.

Growth & Innovation

We're creative, forward-thinking, and always improving — because our mission is to make life better for seniors and the family caregivers who love them.

Always Available

We're here when you need us: 24 hours a day, seven days a week, 365 days a year.

Our promise

"We will care for your loved one with the same respect, kindness, and dignity we would want for our own family."

Home isn't just where someone lives — it's where memories, comfort, and identity live. We're honored to help our clients stay there, safely and confidently, for as long as possible.

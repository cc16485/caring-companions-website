2017 · Where it began

A family decides home shouldn't be negotiable.

Caring Companions was founded with a heartfelt mission: help seniors and adults who need support keep living safely, comfortably, and with dignity in the place that means the most to them. We had watched too many people moved into facilities before they were ready, not because they couldn't live at home, but because they didn't have the right support.

The years since · Earning trust

Growing the way trust grows: one family telling another.

Word spread from family to family across Southwest Missouri. We grew from a handful of caregivers into a team serving six counties, and we kept the rules that made us different: caregivers are our screened, insured, bonded employees, never contractors, and care plans are built around each person's routines, preferences, and story.

Today · Every level of support

From a friendly weekly visit to around-the-clock care.

Families trust us for flexible support at every stage: Wellness Care, Personal Care, Overnight, Live-In, and 24-Hour Care. Our caregivers don't just assist with tasks, they bring companionship, encouragement, and the reassurance that someone is truly there.

2026 · A family of care

Care wherever home is.

Caring Companions has grown into a family of care: in-home caregivers here in Southwest Missouri, HomeTogether TV for staying close through the television anywhere in the country, and HomeTogether Local for finding and hiring caregivers directly. Different doors, one promise.

Our Employees

Every caregiver is thoroughly screened, insured, and bonded, and they're our employees, not contractors. We handle the taxes, insurance, and backup support whenever it's needed.

Family Owned

We're not a franchise. Caring Companions is family owned, and our experience, compassion, and quality of care run higher because of that personal touch.

Growth & Innovation

We're creative, forward-thinking, and always improving, because our mission is to make life better for seniors and the family caregivers who love them.

Always Available

We're here when you need us: 24 hours a day, seven days a week, 365 days a year.

Caregiver Resource Directory

The Ozarks are full of help for seniors and the families caring for them, but it’s scattered across a dozen agencies. We gathered it here, in one place, so you don’t have to. You do not have to be our client to use any of it.

If you need help right now

Emergency or immediate danger: 911 (for a missing person with dementia, call immediately, there is no waiting period).
Emotional crisis, for you or them: call or text 988, free, 24/7. Caregivers use it too.
Sudden confusion or a fast change: call their healthcare provider today.
Suspected abuse, neglect, or exploitation: Missouri Adult Protective Services, (800) 392-0210.
Alzheimer’s Association 24/7 Helpline: (800) 272-3900.

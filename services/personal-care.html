What is Personal Care?

Personal Care builds on our Wellness Care level, adding hands-on assistance with bathing, dressing, toileting, transfers, walking, and feeding, delivered by caregivers trained to preserve independence and dignity, not just complete a task list.

Who it's for

✓  Those needing hands-on help with bathing, dressing, or toileting

✓  People with mobility challenges needing transfer assistance

✓  Families whose loved one can no longer safely self-manage ADLs

✓  Anyone stepping up from Wellness Care as needs increase

Private Duty Nursing

Skilled nursing care, at home.

One-on-one visits from licensed RNs and LPNs for medication management, chronic condition monitoring, and post-surgical care — the clinical layer alongside our non-medical caregiving.

What is Private Duty Nursing?

Registered Nurses (RNs) and Licensed Practical Nurses (LPNs) make weekly or bi-weekly visits for skilled tasks that go beyond what a non-medical caregiver can provide — medication management, wound care oversight, and monitoring chronic conditions. It's often paired with Personal Care or Specialized Care for a complete plan.

Who it's for

✓  Clients needing medication management beyond reminders

✓  Those managing chronic conditions like diabetes or heart failure at home

✓  Post-surgical patients needing wound care oversight

✓  Families wanting a licensed nurse involved in the care plan

Pricing

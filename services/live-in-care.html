Live-In Care

One consistent caregiver, day and night.

A cost-effective alternative to a nursing home or assisted living — a caregiver lives in the home for multiple consecutive days, providing continuity and companionship around the clock.

What is Live-In Care?

A single caregiver typically works 2–5 consecutive days per week, living in the home and providing care throughout the day with a sleeping period overnight. A rotating team of about 3 caregivers covers a full week — fewer faces than 24-Hour Care's shift model, which builds more familiarity and trust over time.

Who it's for

✓  Those who want one familiar caregiver rather than a rotating shift team

✓  Families comparing cost against assisted living or a nursing home

✓  People who need overnight presence but not constant hands-on wakefulness

✓  Clients who value continuity and a consistent daily routine

Hourly Care

Flexible support, on your schedule.

A caregiver comes for a set number of hours on the days and times that work for your family, no long-term contracts, no minimum weekly hours.

Start the Care Assessment Ask Cara if this fits

What is Hourly Care?

Visits of 4 or more hours, ranging from homemaker help to companionship to hands-on personal care, scheduled around your family's routine. It's often the starting point on the care continuum, and can grow into Personal Care, Overnight, or 24-Hour Care as needs change.

Who it's for

✓  Families needing a few hours of help a few days a week

✓  Those wanting to try home care before committing to a larger plan

✓  People needing daytime support while a family caregiver works

✓  Anyone whose needs are still light, companionship, meals, errands

Wellness Care

Connection, conversation, and peace of mind.

For those who are largely independent but shouldn't be alone all day - social interaction, light help around the house, and a familiar face to check in on them.

What is Wellness Care?

Part of our Wellness Care level, companionship, conversation, light housekeeping, meal preparation, and help with errands or transportation. No hands-on personal care is involved; it's about reducing isolation and providing a watchful, friendly presence.

Who it's for

✓  Older adults who live alone and want regular company

✓  Families wanting a watchful presence without full personal care

✓  Those who need help with errands, meals, or light housekeeping

✓  Anyone easing into home care for the first time

What is Overnight Care?

A caregiver lives with and cares for your loved one on an ongoing basis, scheduled in 24–72 hour shifts, typically 3 or more days a week. It sits between Hourly Care and full Live-In or 24-Hour Care — ideal when nighttime safety is the main concern but round-the-clock daytime supervision isn't yet needed.

Who it's for

✓  Families most worried about nighttime falls or wandering

✓  Those who manage daytime care themselves but need overnight relief

✓  People recently discharged who need overnight monitoring temporarily

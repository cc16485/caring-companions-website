Light Housekeeping

A tidy home is a safer, happier home.

Our caregivers keep everyday living spaces clean and clutter-free — which reduces fall hazards, eases stress, and lifts the spirit.

Book a Free Consultation Ask Cara if this fits

What's included

✓ Dusting, vacuuming, and general tidying
✓ Dishes and kitchen cleanup
✓ Laundry, folding, and changing bed linens
✓ Taking out trash and recycling
✓ Keeping walkways clear of trip hazards
✓ Organizing so daily essentials are easy to reach

Why it matters

Light housekeeping isn’t just about appearances. A clean, uncluttered home has fewer things to trip over, makes it easier to move around safely, and simply feels better to live in. Our caregivers fold it into the day so your loved one’s home stays comfortable and safe without them lifting a finger.

Why professionals refer to us

Caregivers are our employees

Every caregiver is screened, background-checked, insured, and bonded, W-2 employees, not contractors. We carry the liability, taxes, and backup coverage.

Fast, reliable starts

We can often assess and begin care the same or next day, including nights and weekends, so your discharge plan doesn't stall.

Available 24/7/365

A real person answers day or night. We staff overnight, live-in, and around-the-clock care, and we cover call-offs so families are never left without help.

We help unlock funding

At no cost to the family, we help apply for Missouri Medicaid HCBS waivers, VA Aid & Attendance, and the Medicare GUIDE dementia-respite benefit (up to 75 covered hours/year).

We keep you in the loop

With the family's consent, you get confirmation that care started and a point of contact for updates, so you can close the loop on your patient with confidence.

Broad coverage

We serve Springfield and 75+ communities across Greene, Christian, Webster, Taney, Douglas, Phelps, and Polk counties.

Planning Ahead

Your parent is doing fine today. Let's plan for tomorrow.

Families who plan ahead have a much easier time when something does change. A few questions will help you know what signs to watch for.

Have you talked with them about future care yet?

Your Planning Ahead Kit

Early signs worth watching for

✓  Skipped bills, expired food, or a less-tidy home than usual

✓  New bruises, or a first fall

✓  Repeating questions or getting confused about time

✓  Withdrawing from hobbies or friends

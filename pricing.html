Transparent Pricing

Our rates, posted right here.

Why do we post our cost of care when many providers don't? Because families have a lot to weigh, and cost is one of it. We believe you shouldn't have to call five agencies just to get a straight answer.

Hourly Care (Live-Out)

Hourly plans ranging from homemaker to companion and personal care.

$28–$34/hour

✓ 4 hours minimum per visit
✓ Billed hourly on the schedule you build
✓ Caregivers follow a detailed service plan of the client's needs
✓ Multiple caregivers may cover longer split shifts

24-Hour Care (Live-Out)

Round-the-clock in-home support through rotating caregiver shifts.

$28–$32/hour

✓ Typically two 12-hour shifts, or three 8-hour shifts, per day
✓ Someone awake and present at all times
✓ A consistent team who gets to know your loved one

Live-In Care (Live-In)

A popular, cost-effective alternative to a nursing home or assisted living.

$450/day

✓ Includes a $25/day caregiver meal fee
✓ 3–7 days per week
✓ Caregivers typically work 2–5 consecutive days
✓ A team of ~3 caregivers per week (vs. 3 per day with 24-Hour Care)

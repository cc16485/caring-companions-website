Brain Injury & Stroke Recovery

Home care after a brain injury or stroke.

Recovering from a stroke or brain injury takes time, patience, and steady support at home. Our caregivers help with the daily work of recovery so your loved one can heal in familiar surroundings.

Book a Free ConsultationAsk Cara

After a stroke or brain injury, the first weeks and months at home shape the whole recovery. Families juggle therapy exercises, medication schedules, mobility and transfer safety, communication changes, and simple fatigue. A trained caregiver provides hands-on help with daily activities, encourages the therapy plan, watches for warning signs, and gives family caregivers room to breathe — all built around your loved one's specific needs.
